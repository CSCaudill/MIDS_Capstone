We provide useful tools that match your own views against those of current senators, as well as against upcoming Senate candidates. Moreover, we provide useful information on candidates like probability of winning, battleground state designations, and likely return on donation. Project Overview

Motivation

We want to make it easier for people, just like you, to participate in politics. We think it's important that people vote based on what matters to them, rather than down party lines.

What it does

Our tool lets you discover candidates that share your values and gives you options to shape our future -- whether by voting for candidates you discover or donating to campaigns that support your values.
