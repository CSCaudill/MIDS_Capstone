PERSONALIZING POLITICS

ABOUT PERSONALIZING POLITICS

Congressmen are the foundation of policy-making in our country, but only 60-70% of eligible voters turn out for presidential elections -- even less (about 40%) turn out for midterm elections.

Many of us sit and complain about the state of the country, so why aren't we more interested in getting the right people elected?

Congressmen aren't generally glamorous. Just like we talk about the CEO of a company, it's the employees who do the day-to-day work and determine how of the vision, not the CEO. We probably hear and know more about celebrities and what their political opinions than our local Congressional representative, so how can we elect someone to represent us if we don't even know who they are?

We are a group of data science students, and we believe that anyone can make a change.

By leveraging this tool, you will be presented with a personalized data-driven analysis that will provide you with the knowledge you need to make an informed decision about how should be our country's leaders. By providing your stance on a number of key political, social, and economic topics, we can align you with the candidates that are the best fit for you, their likelihood of a successful election, & how you may be able to impact their candidacy. Additionally, we'd like to also use this as an opportunity to show you how today's leaders stand up to your views. Armed with this knowledge, you will gain an unbiased understanding of the current state of our country's leaders and how that may look tomorrow.
